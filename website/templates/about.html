Hi there!


Welcome to my site! I am an actor, dancer, singer, choreographer, and visionary based in New York City.

Born and raised in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, my love for acting and theatre sparked from watching Judy Garland in The Wizard of Oz. My love for that classic story, and its lessons, has since grown into an expansive collection beginning with my own Dorothy Gale costume (ruby slippers and all) worn at my preschool graduation. Shortly after, I began my dance training at the age of three and have been movin' and groovin' ever since.

I am a recent summa cum laude graduate of Wagner College, with a B.A. in Theatre Performance and a Minor in Dance. In addition to honing my skills as a performer at Wagner, I also fell in love with the art of creating movement and discovering the healing powers it possesses as a choreographer. It was in the halls of Wagner that I truly began to foster and grow into my essence - altruistic, zany, ray of sunshine, radiating enthusiasm for life, and striving to leave a little whimsy everywhere I go. I am also passionate about being involved in wildy creative theatre that opens our minds to being in the world and of the world - not to stand aside and watch it pass by.

Happy to have you here!

- Em

Aside from making art, you can find me collecting vinyl records, watching an Audrey Hepburn movie, indulging in a black raspberry/vanilla soft serve twist with rainbow sprinkles, walking in between the shelves of a bookstore, and hanging with my pup Kipper.
